# stubbletrade
While farmers desperately burn stubble, industries desperately need it. Biomass power plants, bio-CNG companies, paper mills, mushroom farms — all require crop residue. The supply exists. The demand exists. We connect them.


Every November, Delhi becomes a gas chamber. Schools shut down. Hospitals overflow. The Air Quality Index crosses 999. And somewhere in Punjab, a helpless farmer strikes a match — not because he wants to, but because he has absolutely no other option.

India generates over 500 million tonnes of crop residue every year. Farmers have only 15–20 days between rice harvest and wheat sowing. With no affordable machinery, no buyer, and no logistics support, they burn 92 million tonnes of stubble annually — causing 66,000 premature deaths and ₹30,000 crore in health damage every single year.

But here is the irony. While farmers desperately burn stubble, industries desperately need it. Biomass power plants, bio-CNG companies, paper mills, mushroom farms, and cattle feed manufacturers all require crop residue as raw material. The supply exists. The demand exists. What is missing is the bridge.

StubbleTrade is that bridge.

We are a B2B marketplace that connects farmers with industries through organized collection, baling, and logistics. A farmer simply gives a missed call or sends a WhatsApp message. We send baling machines to the field, collect the stubble at no cost to the farmer, transport it to the nearest industrial buyer, and guarantee payment within 48 hours.

Our sales strategy uses village-level aggregators, FPO partnerships, mandi registration counters, and WhatsApp-based onboarding for farmers. For industry buyers, we deploy direct B2B sales, free pilot programs, and LinkedIn outreach. Our marketing uses powerful rural campaigns — wall paintings, radio ads, farmer testimonials, and the tagline "Parali mat jalao, paisa kamao."

Year one target: 50,000 tonnes traded, ₹1.5 crore revenue, and 5,000 burning incidents prevented.

The farmer is not the villain. The system is. StubbleTrade fixes the system. We don't just trade stubble — we trade pollution for prosperity.

Don't burn it. Earn it.
